Doers wanted! If you want to learn it, play it or just try it, this is the place.

At last count, Allegheny County offered over 200 different programs across our nine amaazing parks. From stand up paddleboarding to mountain biking to soccer leagues to skiing to once-in-a-lifetime experiences with our rangers, we've got countless ways to have fun, keep moving, connect with friends and satisfy your competitive streak

So get out here and sign up for something in a park or two you haven't been to yet. We guarantee it'll be worth the trip, even if you might have to cross an extra river to get there!
